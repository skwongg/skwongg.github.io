    What is the difference between Margin, Border and Padding?

    The margin represents the space outside of a border that is completely transparent and does not have background color. The border is between the margin and the padding and is the most outter layer which can be modified according to style, color and size. The padding is the area cleared between the actual content and the border. Below is a visual representation of the margin, border and padding. "Content" can be text, pictures, etc.
